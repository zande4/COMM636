About Work

Stopmotion Animation

This animation was produced by manipulating paper cutouts and a four-limbed paper doll and capturing frames using the Dragonframe software. The narrative follows a girl who is disillusioned with her circumstances and finds peace in the vastness of nature.

3D Animation

This animation is a 3 minute narrative following the wheel of a roller skate that finds itself lost in the sewers and struggles to find its way home. Created in Blender.

Sewing Animation

This animation follows the procedure of sewing a garment in close, technical detail. The viewer experiences the process at a very close scale and slow pace, inviting them to consider the labor that produced their own clothing. Created in Adobe Animate.

Course Planner

This course planning tool was created for students in the communications program at UNC. It has a completely interactive course selection system that calculates degree progress as users make selections. Created with HTML and JavaScript.

Digital Collage

The photos within this collage were selected from my camera roll and span almost a decade. With my hometown being near New Orleans, this image evokes a Southern, nostalgic feeling. Created in Photoshop.
